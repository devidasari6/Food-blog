                <h2>How to Make the Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies</h2>
                <p><strong>Author:</strong> Devi Dasari</p>
                <p><strong>Date:</strong> August, 2024</p>
                <img src="./images/chocolate cookie.webp" width="200px" height="200px" alt="Chocolate Chip Cookies">
                <p>Nothing beats the smell of freshly baked chocolate chip cookies. In this blog post, I'll walk you through my favorite recipe for chewy, gooey cookies that are always a hit with family and friends.</p>
                
                <h3>Ingredients</h3>
                <ul>
                    <li>1 cup unsalted butter</li>
                    <li>1 cup white sugar</li>
                    <li>1 cup packed brown sugar</li>
                    <li>2 large eggs</li>
                    <li>2 teaspoons vanilla extract</li>
                    <li>3 cups all-purpose flour</li>
                    <li>1 teaspoon baking soda</li>
                    <li>2 cups chocolate chips</li>
                </ul>

                <h3>Instructions</h3>
                <ol>
                    <li>Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
                    <li>Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.</li>
                    <li>Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.</li>
                    <li>Dissolve baking soda in hot water, and add to batter with salt.</li>
                    <li>Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.</li>
                    <li>Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.</li>
                    <li>Bake for 10 minutes, or until edges are nicely browned.</li>
                </ol>
